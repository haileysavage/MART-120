Summary

*Charlotte's Web* is a children's novel written by E.B. White. It tells the story of a pig named Wilbur and his friendship with a barn spider named Charlotte.

Artwork

A visual homage to E.B. White's beloved tale, highlighting the bond between Wilbur and Charlotte.
