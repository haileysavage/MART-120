Summary

*Charlotte's Web* is a children's novel written by E.B. White. It tells the story of a pig named Wilbur and his friendship with a barn spider named Charlotte. When Wilbur learns he will be slaughtered for meat, Charlotte devises a plan to save him by writing words in her web to convince the farmer of his uniqueness.

Artwork

A visual homage to E.B. White's beloved tale, highlighting the bond between Wilbur and Charlotte.
